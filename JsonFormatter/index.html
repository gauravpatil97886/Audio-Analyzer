origin story
Why this tool exists
While debugging a production API response, Saurabh Yadav from the Tech Support team flagged a recurring pain point — extracting specific field values across large JSON arrays was tedious and time-consuming. Every time a support issue came in, the team had to manually scan through hundreds of records just to pull out IDs, statuses, or names. Gaurav Patil built this tool over a weekend to solve exactly that — one click, any key, all values instantly.
